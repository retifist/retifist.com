# 01 — Bow on strap — real-time glue

Single hands-on glue build. Download the bow packet, cut the pieces, glue every seam with full curl waits on camera, build a strap loop with an overlap seam (no snaps), attach the bow, and slide it onto the platform heel.

This is the first real-time glue session on purpose — waits stay in the video so you learn what the cement actually does.

## Session shape

About 90 minutes at the desk (30 minutes buffer + ~60 minutes work). Published video aims for 25–35 minutes with honest wait times.
