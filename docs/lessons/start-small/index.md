# Start Small

Most people quit latex before a finished piece because they bought too much gear or glued in a space that fought them. This collection stays small on purpose: half-meter sheet orders, a small glue tin, and one desk with a cutting mat.

**You will build:** a bow-on-strap entry piece, then a fitted latex sock over a platform ankle-height heel you already own.

**Safety posture:** open windows, decant only a small amount of thinner for the session, and keep the main bottle capped away from the glue zone. See [Safety](/safety/) before opening solvents.
